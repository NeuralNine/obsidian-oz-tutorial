# Summary

Anthropic has raised $30 billion in Series G funding at a $380 billion post-money valuation, led by GIC and Coatue with participation from major investors including Sequoia, Fidelity, Goldman Sachs, and others. The round also includes portions of previously announced investments from Microsoft and NVIDIA.

The company reports a run-rate revenue of $14 billion, growing over 10x annually for three consecutive years. Claude has become the intelligence platform of choice for enterprises and developers, with eight of the Fortune 10 now customers. The number of customers spending over $100,000 annually has grown 7x in the past year, and over 500 businesses now spend over $1 million annually with Anthropic.

Claude Code, made available in May 2025, has grown to over $2.5 billion in run-rate revenue and recently doubled its weekly active users since January. Enterprise use now represents over half of Claude Code revenue. An analysis estimated that 4% of all GitHub public commits worldwide are authored by Claude Code.

The company also launched Cowork, bringing Claude Code capabilities to broader knowledge work tasks with eleven open-source plugins. Their newest model, Opus 4.6, powers agents for real-world work and leads on the GDPval-AA benchmark for economically valuable knowledge work.

- Claude Code run-rate revenue exceeds $2.5B, representing ~4% of GitHub commits
